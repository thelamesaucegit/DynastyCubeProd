About The Dynasty Cube

How The League Works



In The Dynasty Cube, Teams of players draft through the history of Magic, beginning with (nearly) all of the first 500 or so unique cards contained in the earliest Magic sets, and replacing undrafted cards with new cards in chronological set order as The League goes on. 

Drafts are done rotisserie style through a Google Sheet, and typically last for up to two weeks. Each Team will be matched up against one other Team in The League per week, with games to be played via Cockatrice. After 5 weekly matchups, the top Teams face off in a playoff bracket to determine a Champion. 
As each Season progresses, the decisions made by each Team continue having an affect on The League. After each Season, Teams will vote on new rules to enact and other changes that will take effect in the next Season.

Season 1 of The Dynasty Cube began on Oct 24th 2024 and completed December 28th with the Theros Demigods claiming the Championship! 
Season 2 began January 9th 2025 and completed March 25th with the Innistrad Creeps claiming the Championship!
